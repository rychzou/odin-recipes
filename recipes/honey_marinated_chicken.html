Honey Marinated Chicken

Description

The easiest chicken recipe ever! Perfect for lazy days and parties alike. You can use any type of chicken you want: thighs, legs, breasts, etc. Just soak and let the marinade do the work.

Ingredients

  • -30ml soy sauce -1tbsp of honey per chicken breast or per 2 chicken legs -pinch of salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Honey

Directions

  1. Make you marinade by mixing your soy sauce (15ml per 1 Tbsp of honey), honey (1 Tbsp per breast or per 2 chicken legs for reference), salt, black pepper, garlic, and paprika in a bowl or zip lock bag.
  2. Cut slits into your chicken to help absorb the marinade and place in the same container as marinade
  3. Let marinade for 30 minutes minimum
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees for legs and thighs and 350 degrees for breasts
  5. Turn after 40 minutes for legs and thighs and 30 minutes for breasts
  6. Cook for 10 more minutes
